# Website Project Proposal

 FlowMaster Plumbing

Overview
FlowMaster Plumbing is a small, professional plumbing business offering residential and small-commercial plumbing services, including leak detection and repair, new installations, and 24/7 emergency call-outs. The website will serve as the primary digital front door for new clients, showcase services, provide a point of contact, and enable customers to send enquiries quickly.

History
FlowMaster Plumbing was founded in 2016 by a licensed plumber with 12 years' industry experience. The company grew from local word-of-mouth referrals to a small team of three licensed technicians and a customer-service coordinator. FlowMaster has a reputation for quick response times and transparent pricing.
