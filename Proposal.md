# Website Project Proposal

## Proposal A (Selected): FlowMaster Plumbing

Overview
FlowMaster Plumbing is a small, professional plumbing business offering residential and small-commercial plumbing services, including leak detection and repair, new installations, and 24/7 emergency call-outs. The website will serve as the primary digital front door for new clients, showcase services, provide a point of contact, and enable customers to send enquiries quickly.

History
FlowMaster Plumbing was founded in 2016 by a licensed plumber with 12 years' industry experience. The company grew from local word-of-mouth referrals to a small team of three licensed technicians and a customer-service coordinator. FlowMaster has a reputation for quick response times and transparent pricing.

Target Audience
- Homeowners aged 28–65 within the Cape Town metro area.
- Landlords and property managers requiring reliable maintenance services.
- Small business owners requiring plumbing installations or urgent repairs.

Goals
1. Present FlowMaster Plumbing professionally, increasing trust and conversions.
2. Provide clear, accessible information about services and pricing (where appropriate).
3. Offer a simple enquiry flow for customers to request work or emergency assistance.
4. Present a map and clear contact channels to encourage calls and visits.

## Proposal B (Alternative): The Sweet Spot Bakery

Overview
The Sweet Spot Bakery is an artisan bakery specializing in sourdough loaves, pastries, and custom celebration cakes. The proposed website is a visual, appointment-driven site that showcases the product range and enables custom cake enquiries.

History
Established in 2019, The Sweet Spot started as a market stall and grew into a small storefront with a loyal local following. It focuses on high-quality ingredients and seasonal menus.

Target Audience
- Food-loving locals aged 20–55.
- Event planners and customers ordering bespoke cakes.
- Market visitors and casual passers-by seeking daily bakery items.

Goals
1. Showcase high-quality photography of baked goods.
2. Provide a simple order/enquiry form for custom cakes.
3. Increase footfall to the store and social engagement.

### Planning Details (FlowMaster only)

Sitemap
- Home (index.html)
- About (about.html)
- Services (services.html)
- Enquiry (enquiry.html)
- Contact (contact.html)

Wireframe Descriptions
Home (index.html)
- Header: Top-level navigation with company logo on the left and navigation links on the right. On mobile, a hamburger icon appears.
- Hero Section: Large hero image with headline and CTA.
- Featured Services: Grid of 3 service cards.
- About Preview: Short paragraph and link to about page.
- Footer: Contact info and quick links.

About (about.html)
- Intro: Company history and mission.
- Team Section: Grid with headshots and bios.
- Certifications/Guarantees: Icons and short statements.
- CTA: Link to enquiry and contact pages.

Contact (contact.html)
- Two-column layout with contact form and map/location details.

Technical Specs
- HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
- Leaflet.js for maps
- Google Fonts: Roboto
- Hosting: GitHub Pages
